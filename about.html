Our Product Range

At GR BAGS, we pride ourselves on offering a diverse array of polybags to meet the varied needs of our clients. Our product range includes both standard and specialized bags, ensuring we have the right solution for every packaging requirement.

  • Standard Polybags: Our standard polybags come in a variety of sizes, each designed to offer durability and reliability for your everyday packaging needs.
  • Laminated, Unlaminated, Gusseted & Printed Carry Bags with Punched Handle: These versatile carry bags are available in various configurations to suit different applications. Sizes available: 12” x 12”, 12” x 16”, 14” x 16”, 14” x 20”, 16” x 20”.
  • Laminated, Unlaminated, Gusseted & Printed Sacks: Perfect for larger packaging needs, offering a variety of options in sizes ranging from 19” x 27” to 28” x 47”.
  • Big Packing Bags: Ideal for bulk packaging requirements, available in sizes like 38” x 48” and 45” x 50”.
  • Specialized Bags: Including multicolor options, transparent bags, and leno bags, catering to specific packaging needs.
